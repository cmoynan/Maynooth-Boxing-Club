Who We Are

Founded in 2019 Maynooth Boxing club was started by John Donovan and his wife Marlene. Both John and Marlene have competed at amateur level and both represented Ireland at numerous world Championships. John and Marlene are both from Maynooth. When they began boxing training they had to travel to Dublin city as there were no local boxing clubs close by. Since both have retired from competitive boxing their goal has been to setup a local boxing club in Maynooth. This became a reality in 2018 and since then they opened a fully equipped boxing gym that provides classes for all ages and all skill levels. Whether your goal is to just get fit or learn the beautiful science of boxing this is the gym that will cater for you.They gym operates on a pay as you go system so there is no commitment at all required ad prices range depending on the classes that you attend. The first class for everyone is free. So come on down or get in touch using our contact form and we will get back to you.
